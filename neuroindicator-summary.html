üì¶ What We Built

1. Neuroindicator Screening Tool

Single HTML file - Fully portable, no dependencies

  • 15 evidence-based questions covering stress, anxiety, sleep, mood, focus
  • 7 outcome pathways - Crisis, baseline wellness, stress/anxiety, mood, attention/focus, general support
  • ClearMinds branding - Teal/cream color scheme
  • PDF generation - Downloadable GP summaries
  • Mobile responsive - Works on all devices
  • Ethical design - "You're fine" is valid outcome, transparent about commercial relationships

2. Clinical Rationale Document

Comprehensive explanation of question design and sequencing

  • Clinical evidence base (PHQ-9, GAD-7, ASRS, ISI, PSS)
  • Question-by-question breakdown
  • Sequencing strategy (funnel approach, safety-first)
  • Comparison to validated scales
  • Clinical validation plan

3. Question Export for Quizzle

Plain text format with all questions, options, and scoring logic

  • All 15 questions with exact wording
  • 7 outcome pathways detailed
  • Implementation notes for Quizzle team
  • Warning about probabilistic scoring complexity

4. Updated Corporate Governance Framework

Simplified and toned down to reflect wellness positioning

  • Simplified from 6 to 4 components

üé® Key Design Principles

Ethical Framework

  • Safety-first: Crisis screening at Q2 (early intervention)
  • Balanced recommendations: Non-commercial options prominent
  • "You're fine" is valid: Not just a sales funnel
  • Transparent: Commercial relationships clearly flagged
  • Privacy-first: Browser-only processing, optional email

Clinical Foundation

  • Evidence-based questions (validated screening tools)
  • Multi-dimensional assessment (8 dimensions tracked)
  • Contextual interpretation (duration, impact, attempts)
  • Appropriate escalation (GP, specialist, crisis services)
  • Probabilistic scoring (not boolean logic)

üìä Technical Specifications

Question Bank

Count Time Type Evidence Base
15 questions 5-10 minutes Mix of scales & multiple choice PHQ-9, GAD-7, ASRS, ISI, PSS

Dimensions Tracked (8 total)

  1. Crisis/safety
  2. Baseline wellness
  3. Stress/anxiety
  4. Sleep/energy
  5. Attention/focus
  6. Mood regulation
  7. Somatic symptoms
  8. Life stressors

Scoring Logic

Probabilistic (not boolean)

  • Multiple dimensions scored simultaneously
  • Signal types: direct, inverse, moderate, contextual
  • Thresholds: Low (0-2.5), Medium (2.5-3.5), High (3.5+)
  • Outcome based on highest confidence + contextual modifiers
