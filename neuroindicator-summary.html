üì¶ What We Built (V2.0)

1. Simplified 5-Question Check-In

Reduced from 15 clinical questions to 5 conversational questions

  • Q1: How are you feeling overall?
  • Q2: What's your main reason for checking in?
  • Q3: How long has this been going on?
  • Q4: Have you tried anything to help?
  • Q5: Crisis check (thoughts of self-harm)

Time: 2 minutes (down from 5-10 minutes)

Tone: Conversational wellness check, not clinical screening

2. Community-First Results Page

Lead with 91,000-member community, not clinical pathways

  • Primary CTA: "Enter Community Now" (always first)
  • Secondary: Personalized pathway based on Q2
  • Tertiary: OCEAN assessment (for optimization users)
  • Optional: GP summary (for clinical escalation)

Positioning: Community is where most healing happens

3. OCEAN Personality Assessment Integration

Gateway to deeper self-understanding

  • High prominence: For "wellness/optimization" users (Q1: doing well, Q2: understand myself better, Q3: not applicable, Q4: optimize)
  • Low prominence: For struggling users ("available when you're feeling better")
  • Future retention tool: Email campaigns Week 2-4 promoting OCEAN

4. De-Clinicalized Language

Removed all clinical/medical positioning

  • ‚ùå "Neuroindicator Screening" ‚Üí ‚úÖ "Wellness Check-In"
  • ‚ùå "Clinical assessment" ‚Üí ‚úÖ "Starting point"
  • ‚ùå "PHQ-9, GAD-7, ASRS, ISI" ‚Üí ‚úÖ (removed from user-facing copy)
  • ‚ùå "Developed with Dr. Thomas Dannhauser" ‚Üí ‚úÖ (removed due to conflict of interest)

5. Intelligent Routing Logic

8 pathways based on user responses:

  • Crisis: Immediate safety resources + community
  • Wellness/Optimization: Community + OCEAN (high prominence)
  • Work Stress: Community + stress hypnotherapy + Edward (AI coach)
  • Sleep: Community + sleep programs + Kainne
  • Anxiety: Community + anxiety sessions + Lucy
  • Mood: Community + mood support + Tom + GP summary
  • Focus: Community + focus sessions + Alastair + clinical partner
  • Habits: Community + targeted hypnotherapy

üé® Key Design Principles

Community-First, Not Crisis-Only

  • Old EAP model: "Call when desperate" ‚Üí isolating
  • Clear Minds model: "Join 91k members" ‚Üí welcoming
  • Result: 30-40% engagement vs 5-10% traditional EAP

De-Stigmatized Entry Point

  • Not: "Take a mental health screening"
  • Instead: "How are you doing right now?"
  • Barrier removed: Feels like checking in with a friend, not admitting you need help

OCEAN as Retention & Premium Engagement

  • Wellness check-in: Triage + onboarding (2 minutes)
  • OCEAN: Deep self-discovery (20-30 minutes)
  • Strategy: Hook with quick check-in, retain with OCEAN
  • B2B value: Aggregated OCEAN insights = unique employer reporting
