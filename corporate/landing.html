Three-Pathway Support Model via Smart Start Minds:

We partner with Smart Start Minds (CQC-registered: 1-2950268195) to provide a stepped care approach:

  • Pathway 1: ClearMinds wellness platform (self-directed, NOT medical)
  • Pathway 2: Neuro-evaluator assessment (psychometric insight, NOT diagnosis)
  • Pathway 3: CQC-regulated clinical treatment (medical diagnosis & therapy)

See our Health Service Level Agreement for full pathway details.
