## Purpose of This Policy

This Acceptable Use Policy sets out the rules governing your use of Clear Minds services. By using our platform, you agree to use it responsibly, lawfully, and in accordance with these rules. Violations may result in suspension or termination of your account.

---

## 1. General Principles

When using Clear Minds, you must:

- **Use services lawfully** – Comply with all applicable UK and international laws and regulations
- **Respect others** – Do not harm, harass, or interfere with other users (especially in community spaces)
- **Be honest** – Provide accurate information and do not misrepresent yourself
- **Protect security** – Do not compromise the security or integrity of our platform
- **Use responsibly** – Use services for their intended purpose (personal wellbeing and development)

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

Clear Minds services are intended for:

- **Personal wellbeing** – Accessing hypnotherapy, meditation, and breathwork for your own use
- **Stress management and relaxation** – Supporting general mental health and wellbeing
- **Personal development** – Self-improvement and personal growth activities
- **Workplace wellness** – If provided by your employer as a benefit
- **Peer support** – Optional participation in moderated Facebook community for shared wellness journeys

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

### You Must NOT Use Clear Minds For:

- **Illegal activities** – Any unlawful purpose or in violation of any laws
- **Harm to yourself or others** – If you are at risk of harm, seek immediate professional help (see Medical Disclaimer)
- **Commercial purposes** – Reselling, redistributing, or commercially exploiting our content
- **Unauthorised access** – Attempting to access areas of the platform not intended for you
- **System interference** – Disrupting, damaging, or impairing our services or infrastructure
- **Providing medical/clinical advice** – Clear Minds is NOT a medical service; do not present it as such

---

## 4. Account Security and Integrity

### 4.1 Account Responsibility

You must:

- **Keep your password secure** – Do not share your login credentials with anyone
- **Provide accurate information** – Name and email must be truthful and current
- **Use your own account only** – Do not access or use another person's account
- **Notify us of unauthorised access** – Report any security breaches immediately to support@clearminds.com
- **One account per person** – Do not create multiple accounts for yourself

### 4.2 Prohibited Account Activities

You must **not**:

- Share your account with others (except as permitted for under-18s with parental supervision)
- Create accounts using false or misleading information
- Impersonate another person or entity
- Create accounts for fraudulent purposes
- Attempt to circumvent payment or access controls
- Claim to be affiliated with Clear Minds or Concierge Health without authorization

---

## 5. Content and Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Our Content – What You Can Do

You may:

- **Stream content** – Listen to hypnotherapy, meditation, and breathwork sessions within the app
- **Download for personal offline use** – If this feature is available and only within the app
- **Use for personal wellbeing** – Engage with content for your own personal development

### 5.2 Our Content – What You Cannot Do

You must **not**:

- **Copy, reproduce, or distribute** – Record, download (outside the app), copy, or share our audio content, scripts, or materials
- **Publicly perform or display** – Play our content in public settings (e.g., group therapy sessions, yoga classes, commercial venues) without a commercial license
- **Create derivative works** – Modify, adapt, or create new works based on our content
- **Remove copyright notices** – Strip metadata, watermarks, or attribution
- **Reverse engineer** – Decompile, disassemble, or attempt to extract source code
- **Use for commercial purposes** – Monetise, sell, or commercially exploit our content
- **Claim medical endorsement** – Present Clear Minds content as medically prescribed or CQC-regulated (it is not)

## 6. Community Guidelines (Facebook Group)

If you participate in our optional Facebook community:

### 6.1 Community Dos

- **Be supportive and respectful** – Encourage and uplift other members
- **Share your wellness journey** – Personal experiences, tips, and positive stories
- **Maintain confidentiality** – What's shared in the community stays in the community
- **Report concerns** – Alert moderators to concerning posts or behavior

### 6.2 Community Don'ts

You must **not**:

- **Share identifying information** – Do not disclose which employer provides your access (if B2B)
- **Provide medical advice** – Share personal experiences only, not clinical guidance
- **Harass or bully** – Any form of harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior
- **Spam or promote** – Commercial advertising, self-promotion, or spam
- **Share graphic content** – Explicit descriptions of self-harm, suicide, or trauma (trigger warning: this may harm others)
- **Violate Facebook's Community Standards** – All Facebook rules apply

**See Community Terms of Service and Enhanced Moderator Protocols for full details.**

## 7. Technical Restrictions

### 7.1 System Integrity

You must **not**:

- **Use automated systems** – Deploy bots, scrapers, or automated tools to access our services
- **Circumvent security measures** – Bypass authentication, encryption, or access controls
- **Interfere with services** – Overload servers, perform denial-of-service attacks, or disrupt functionality
- **Exploit vulnerabilities** – Deliberately exploit bugs, errors, or security flaws (report them to us instead at security@clearminds.com)
- **Reverse engineer** – Decompile software or attempt to discover source code
- **Access restricted areas** – Attempt to access backend systems, databases, or admin functions

### 7.2 Malicious Activity

You must **not**:

- Upload or transmit viruses, malware, or harmful code
- Introduce any technology that could damage or interfere with our services
- Attempt to gain unauthorised access to our systems, networks, or data
- Conduct security testing or penetration testing without prior written authorisation

## 8. Safe and Responsible Use

### 8.1 Using Hypnotherapy Safely

**For your safety, you must:**

- **Use in a safe environment** – Do NOT use hypnotherapy or meditation while driving, operating machinery, or in situations requiring full attention
- **Stop if you feel unwell** – Discontinue use if you experience adverse effects (dizziness, disorientation, distress)
- **Consult professionals for health concerns** – Do not use Clear Minds as a substitute for medical or mental health treatment (see Medical Disclaimer)
- **Seek emergency help if needed** – If you experience a mental health crisis, call 999, 111, or Samaritans (116 123)
- **Consider clinical referral** – If self-directed wellness is not enough, ask about our Concierge Health referral pathway

### 8.2 Age-Appropriate Use

- **Users must be 16+** – Minimum age requirement
- **Users aged 16-17 require parental consent** – And should use services under parental supervision
- **Content is designed for adults** – Not specifically tailored for children or adolescents

## 9. Privacy and Data Protection

### 9.1 Respecting Privacy

You must:

- **Protect your own privacy** – Be mindful of personal information you choose to share in community spaces
- **Respect others' privacy** – Do not share, distribute, or misuse other users' personal information
- **Comply with data protection laws** – If you are an employer providing Clear Minds to employees, ensure GDPR compliance (see Employer Non-Snooping Agreement)

### 9.2 Employer-Provided Access (B2B)

If you access Clear Minds through your employer:

- **Your usage is confidential** – Your employer does not see your individual session details or usage (see Privacy Policy)
- **Use is voluntary** – You are not required to use Clear Minds and should not feel pressured to do so
- **Do not misuse access** – Use services for personal wellbeing, not for unauthorised commercial purposes
- **Do not attempt to identify other employees** – If you access aggregated reports as an HR representative, respect k≥10 anonymity thresholds

## 10. Reporting Violations

### 10.1 How to Report

If you become aware of any violation of this Acceptable Use Policy, please report it to us immediately:

- **Email:** support@clearminds.com or abuse@clearminds.com
- **Subject:** "AUP Violation Report"
- **Include:** Details of the violation, your account information (if applicable), and any evidence

### 10.2 Community Safety Concerns

If you see concerning posts in the Facebook community:

- **Use Facebook's reporting tools**
- **Tag moderators in the group**
- **Email:** community@clearminds.com

### 10.3 Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability or bug:

- **Do not exploit it** – Immediately cease any testing and do not access or disclose data
- **Report it responsibly** – Contact us at **security@clearminds.com** with details
- **Allow us time to fix it** – Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had time to remediate

---

## 11. Consequences of Violations

### 11.1 Enforcement Actions

If you violate this Acceptable Use Policy, we may take the following actions:

- **Warning** – Notification of the violation and request to cease the activity
- **Community restriction** – Removal from Facebook group (community access only)
- **Account suspension** – Temporary restriction of access to services
- **Account termination** – Permanent closure of your account without refund
- **Legal action** – Reporting to law enforcement or pursuing civil remedies for serious violations (e.g., fraud, hacking, copyright infringement)
- **Referral to authorities** – Cooperation with law enforcement investigations

### 11.2 Factors We Consider

When determining the appropriate response to a violation, we consider:

- The severity and nature of the violation
- Whether it was intentional or accidental
- The potential harm caused to other users or Clear Minds
- Your history of compliance
- Whether you cooperated with our investigation
