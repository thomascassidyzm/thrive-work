Care Quality Governance Framework

Systematic approach to quality, safety, and compliance in digital wellness services

Executive Summary

A Care Quality Governance Framework provides the systematic structure for maintaining and improving the quality, safety, and compliance of healthcare and wellness services. For digital wellness platforms like ClearMinds, it ensures accountability, manages risk, and drives continuous improvement while scaling to serve all users.

This framework integrates clinical best practices with corporate governance, relevant regulatory compliance, and operational excellence‚Äîenabling ClearMinds to deliver high-quality wellness services while preparing for B2B enterprise partnerships at scale.
