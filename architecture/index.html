1. Platform Overview - The Complete Ecosystem

Thrive acts as an intelligent routing platform connecting employees to the right resources based on their assessment results.

Key Insight

Thrive is a platform, not a single product. The assessment layer is the core IP that intelligently routes employees to the right combination of content, coaching, and clinical services.

2. Decision Tree - How Employees Are Routed

Neuroindicator assessment splits employees into three pathways based on their needs and risk level.

Adaptive Routing

Employees can move between pathways based on quarterly pulse results. Someone in wellness tier who shows deterioration can be escalated to clinical support.

3. Privacy Architecture - Data Separation Model

Three separate databases with different access rules ensure employee privacy while providing useful organizational insights.

K-Anonymity Enforcement

Employers see NO data until minimum 10 employees complete assessments. This is enforced at the database level with hard-coded constraints - no bypass possible.
