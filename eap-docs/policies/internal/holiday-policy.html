Holiday Policy

Annual Leave Entitlement & Guidelines

Policy Information

Policy Owner: HR Director

Effective Date: July 2025

Review Date: July 2026

Version: 1.0

Applies To: All Employees

Annual Entitlement

28 days (including Bank Holidays) for full-time employees. Pro-rata for part-time. Increases to 30 days after 5 years' service.

Booking Holiday

  • Request via HR system, manager approval required
  • 2 weeks' notice for single days, 4 weeks for 5+ consecutive days
  • Maximum 2 weeks consecutive unless exceptional circumstances
  • Blackout periods apply during peak business periods
  • Manager discretion to refuse if business needs require

Bank Holidays

8 UK bank holidays included in entitlement. Can be taken on actual day or banked for another time (with agreement).

Carry Over

Up to 5 days may be carried to next year (with manager approval). Must be used by end of March. Unused days forfeited.

Sickness During Holiday

If genuinely ill during holiday, may reclaim days as sick leave (medical certificate required for absences 5+ days).

Leaving Company

Unused holiday paid out. Excess taken deducted from final pay.
