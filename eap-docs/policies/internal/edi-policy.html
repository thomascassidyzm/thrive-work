Equality, Diversity & Inclusion Policy

Creating an Inclusive Workplace for All

Policy Information

Policy Owner: HR Director

Effective Date: July 2025

Review Date: Annual

Version: 1.0

Applies To: All Employees

Our Commitment

ClearMinds is committed to creating an inclusive workplace where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to bring their whole selves to work. We believe diversity strengthens our organisation and enhances the care we provide to our users.

Protected Characteristics

We prohibit discrimination based on:

  • Age
  • Disability
  • Gender reassignment
  • Marriage and civil partnership
  • Pregnancy and maternity
  • Race
  • Religion or belief
  • Sex
  • Sexual orientation

Our Approach

Recruitment: Fair, transparent processes free from bias. Anonymous CV screening where appropriate.

Development: Equal access to training, mentoring, and career progression opportunities for all.

Workplace Culture: Zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or discrimination. Safe reporting mechanisms.

Reasonable Adjustments: Proactive support for disabled employees and those with long-term conditions.

Flexible Working: Supportive of requests that enable work-life balance and accommodate caring responsibilities.

Responsibilities

All Staff: Treat colleagues with respect, challenge inappropriate behaviour, undertake EDI training.

Managers: Lead by example, ensure fair treatment, address concerns promptly, create inclusive teams.

HR: Monitor diversity metrics, investigate complaints, provide EDI guidance and training.

Leadership: Set the tone from the top, resource EDI initiatives, hold managers accountable.

Reporting & Support

If you experience or witness discrimination:

  • Speak to your manager or HR immediately
  • Use the Grievance Procedure if needed
  • Anonymous reporting available via our confidential hotline
  • External support: ACAS, Citizens Advice, Equality Advisory Support Service

Zero Tolerance

Discrimination, harassment, or victimisation will result in disciplinary action up to and including dismissal. We take all reports seriously and investigate thoroughly.
