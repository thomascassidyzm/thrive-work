Grievance & Dispute Policy

Purpose

Fair process for raising and resolving workplace concerns, complaints, or disputes.

Informal Resolution

Try to resolve informally first by speaking with your manager or HR. Most issues can be resolved through open discussion.

Formal Grievance

If informal resolution unsuccessful, submit written grievance to HR stating: issue, when it occurred, desired outcome. Acknowledge within 5 days.

Grievance Meeting

  • Held within 10 working days
  • Right to be accompanied
  • Opportunity to explain concerns
  • Investigation if required
  • Written outcome within 10 days

Appeal

Right to appeal within 5 days if dissatisfied. Heard by senior manager not previously involved. Decision final.

Confidentiality

All grievances handled confidentially. Information shared only on need-to-know basis. No victimisation for raising genuine concerns.
