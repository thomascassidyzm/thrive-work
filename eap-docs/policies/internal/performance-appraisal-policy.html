Performance Appraisal Policy

Purpose

Regular, constructive performance conversations to support development, recognise achievement, and align individual goals with company objectives.

Appraisal Cycle

  • Annual Review: Comprehensive assessment, goal setting, salary review
  • Mid-Year Review: Progress check, adjust goals if needed
  • Monthly 1-2-1s: Ongoing feedback, support, quick wins

Annual Review Process

  • Self-assessment completed by employee
  • Manager prepares assessment
  • Face-to-face meeting (minimum 60 minutes)
  • Discussion of achievements, challenges, development needs
  • SMART goals set for coming year
  • Development plan agreed
  • Written summary within 5 days

Rating Scale

Exceptional | Exceeds | Achieves | Developing | Improvement Required

Link to Development

Performance reviews identify: training needs, stretch assignments, career aspirations, promotion readiness. L&D budget allocated based on appraisal outcomes.

Salary Review

Performance ratings inform annual salary review. Exceptional/Exceeds performers considered for higher increases, promotions, bonuses.
