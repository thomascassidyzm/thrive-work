Hybrid Working Policy

Our Approach

ClearMinds embraces hybrid working, balancing flexibility with collaboration. Default: 2-3 days office, 2-3 days remote per week.

Eligibility

All roles eligible unless: requires physical presence, new starters (first 3 months office-based for onboarding), specific team needs require office presence.

Office Days

  • Team days: Wednesdays (all teams in office)
  • One-to-ones and team meetings scheduled on office days where possible
  • Minimum 2 days/week in office for full-time employees
  • Flexibility for appointments, caring duties (with notice)

Remote Working Expectations

  • Appropriate workspace: quiet, good internet, ergonomic setup
  • Available during core hours (10am-4pm)
  • Respond to messages within reasonable timeframe
  • Video on for meetings
  • Log hours accurately

Equipment

Company provides: laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse. Home office budget £200/year for desk setup improvements.

Health & Safety

DSE assessment required for home workspace. Report issues to facilities manager. Insurance covers remote working.
