Recruitment & Selection Policy

Purpose

Fair, transparent recruitment attracting and selecting the best talent whilst promoting diversity and inclusion.

Principles

  • Merit-based selection: best person for role regardless of background
  • Diversity: Actively seek diverse candidate pools
  • No discrimination based on protected characteristics
  • Transparent process: Clear stages, timely feedback
  • Data protection: Candidate data handled per GDPR

Process

  • Job Analysis: Define role, requirements, competencies
  • Advert: Inclusive language, clear requirements, salary transparency
  • Shortlisting: Against criteria, anonymous CV screening where possible
  • Interviews: Structured questions, diverse panel, candidate experience focus
  • Offer: Conditional on references, right to work checks, DBS if applicable

Internal Candidates

Encouraged to apply. Considered fairly alongside external candidates. Feedback provided regardless of outcome.

Referrals

Employee referrals welcomed (£500 bonus for successful hires). Referred candidates go through full process.
