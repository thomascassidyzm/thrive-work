Training & Development Policy

Commitment

ClearMinds invests in developing our people to their full potential through continuous learning, professional development, and career progression opportunities.

Training Budget

£1,000 per employee annually for external training, conferences, certifications. Additional budget for role-critical training.

Types of Development

  • Induction: Comprehensive onboarding (2-week programme)
  • Role-specific: Skills training for job proficiency
  • Mandatory: Compliance, H&S, GDPR, EDI
  • Professional: External courses, certifications, conferences
  • Leadership: Management training for current/aspiring leaders
  • Personal: Skills beyond current role for career growth

Requesting Training

Discuss in 1-2-1s or appraisals. Submit request form with: course details, cost, business benefit, time required. Manager approval needed. Priority to development plan items.

Study Leave

5 days/year paid leave for exam preparation/attendance. Additional unpaid leave may be approved for degree programmes.

Professional Memberships

Company pays for role-relevant professional body memberships (e.g. BACP, BPS, CIPD).
