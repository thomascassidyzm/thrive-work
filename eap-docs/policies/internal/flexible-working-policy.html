Flexible Working Policy

Eligibility

All employees with 26 weeks' service can request flexible working. Requests considered sympathetically for work-life balance, caring responsibilities, health needs.

Types of Flexibility

  • Part-time hours
  • Flexitime (within core hours)
  • Compressed hours
  • Remote/hybrid working
  • Job sharing
  • Term-time working

Making a Request

Submit written request including: proposed pattern, start date, impact on role, how to manage changes. Manager responds within 8 weeks. Meeting arranged to discuss.

Considerations

Decisions based on: business needs, customer service, team impact, costs, feasibility. Refusals explained with business reasons.

Trial Periods

3-month trial for new arrangements. Review meeting before permanent decision. Can revert if not working.
