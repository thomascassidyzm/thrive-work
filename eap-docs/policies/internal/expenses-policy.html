Expenses Policy

Claiming Expenses

Reasonable business expenses reimbursed within 30 days of claim submission. Submit via expense system with receipts for items over £10.

Allowable Expenses

  • Travel: Mileage at 45p/mile (first 10,000 miles), public transport (standard class), taxis when necessary
  • Accommodation: Up to £150/night (London), £100/night (elsewhere). Book via approved suppliers.
  • Meals: Up to £5 breakfast, £10 lunch, £25 dinner. Alcohol limit: £15
  • Client entertainment: Pre-approved by line manager
  • Equipment: Pre-approved purchases for business use

Non-Allowable

  • Personal items or services
  • Commuting costs to usual place of work
  • Fines or penalties
  • Excessive or luxury expenditure

Submission

Claims within 3 months of expense. Late claims may not be reimbursed. VAT receipts required where applicable. False claims = disciplinary action.
