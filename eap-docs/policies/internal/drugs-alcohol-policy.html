Drugs & Alcohol Policy

Purpose

Maintain a safe, healthy workplace free from substance misuse.

Prohibited

  • Reporting to work under the influence
  • Consuming alcohol/drugs during working hours
  • Possessing/distributing illegal substances

Responsible Drinking

Alcohol at company events: drink responsibly, arrange safe transport, respect non-drinkers.

Prescription Medication

Inform manager if medication impairs work capability. Temporary adjustments arranged.

Support Available

Substance issues treated as health matters. Confidential support through HR, occupational health, counselling. Early disclosure leads to support, not punishment.

Consequences

Being under the influence at work is gross misconduct, potentially leading to dismissal.
