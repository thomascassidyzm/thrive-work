1. Introduction

This Record of Processing Activities (ROPA) documents all processing activities carried out by ClearMinds Ltd as a data controller, as required by Article 30 of the UK General Data Protection Regulation.

This record includes:

  • The purposes of each processing activity
  • Categories of personal data processed
  • Categories of data subjects
  • Categories of recipients of personal data
  • International data transfers (if any)
  • Retention periods
  • Security measures

Processing Activity 1: EAP Service Delivery and Clinical Care

Activity Reference:
PA-001
Purpose of Processing:
• Providing Employee Assistance Programme services
• Mental health assessment and support
• Delivering counselling and therapeutic interventions
• Managing appointments and service access
• Maintaining clinical records
• Ensuring continuity of care
Lawful Basis:
Standard Personal Data: Contract (Article 6(1)(b))
Special Category Data (Health): Explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a)) and Health/social care purposes (Article 9(2)(h))
Categories of Data Subjects:
• Employees of corporate clients
• Contractors and temporary workers (where applicable)
• Immediate family members of employees
• Former employees (where applicable)
Categories of Personal Data:
Standard Personal Data:
• Name, contact details (email, phone, address)
• Date of birth
• Employment reference/employee ID
• Communication records (emails, call logs, chat transcripts)

Special Category Data (Health Data):
• Mental health concerns and symptoms
• Clinical assessments and diagnoses
• Treatment plans and therapy notes
• Progress notes and outcomes
• Related health information affecting mental health
Categories of Recipients:
• Clinical staff providing care (counsellors, therapists, clinical directors)
• Clinical supervisors (for quality assurance - anonymised where possible)
• IT support staff (limited access for technical support only)
• Corporate clients (aggregated, anonymised statistics only - NOT individual session details)
• Healthcare providers (with explicit consent only - e.g., GP referrals)
• Emergency services (in crisis situations only)
• Legal/regulatory authorities (where legally required)
International Transfers:
None - all data processed and stored within the United Kingdom
Retention Period:
• Clinical records (adults): 8 years from last contact
• Clinical records (children/young people): Until 25th birthday OR 8 years from last contact (whichever is longer)
• Account information: Duration of service + 2 years
Security Measures:
• Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
• Multi-factor authentication for all user accounts
• Role-based access controls
• Regular security audits and penetration testing
• Staff confidentiality training
• Secure backup procedures
• Incident response procedures

[Backup service provider - name and location]
