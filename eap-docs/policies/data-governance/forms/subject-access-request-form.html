If acting on behalf of someone else, you MUST provide:

  • Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) document, OR

⏱️ What Happens Next?

  1. We'll acknowledge your request within 3 business days
  2. We may contact you if we need clarification or additional proof of identity
  3. We'll provide your information within one month
  4. If your request is complex, we may extend to 3 months (we'll tell you why)
  5. This service is free of charge

ℹ️ Your Rights

In addition to accessing your data, you also have the right to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (in certain circumstances)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request data portability
  • Complain to the ICO if unhappy with our response

Contact our DPO if you'd like to exercise any of these rights.
