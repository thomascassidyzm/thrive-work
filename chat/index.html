Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the THRIVE assessments?

THRIVE uses pattern recognition, not diagnosis. We reflect back what we notice in your responses and invite you to explore what resonates. The accuracy comes from your honest self-reflection, not our algorithms.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We never share your assessment data or personal information. Your responses are used only to generate your personalized insights and are stored securely.

Can I retake assessments?

Yes! We encourage retaking assessments as you grow and change. It's fascinating to see how your patterns evolve over time.

How is THRIVE different from other personality tests?

THRIVE focuses on behavior patterns, not personality types. We reveal the gap between your conscious values and unconscious actions, giving you specific areas for growth rather than fixed labels.
